## Assembly Order

1. **Back + Bottom** → L-bracket, glue & screw
2. **Add Sides** → Between top/bottom
3. **Install U-Braces** → All 5 at marked positions
4. **Add Top** → Close box
5. **Laminate & Install Baffle** → 3" recess
6. **Mount Drivers** → Gasket tape, bolt down
