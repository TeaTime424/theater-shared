## 5. U-Brace System (Extended Notched Leg Design)

### Design Rationale
Extended legs reach forward to directly support the baffle at 4.5" from front. Notches allow continuous side cleats to pass through, providing:
1. Direct baffle support at driver mounting locations (U-2 and U-4)
2. Continuous perimeter cleat support (no unsupported gaps)
3. Continuous load path from baffle to back panel
4. Maximum stiffness for high-excursion drivers

### U-Brace Dimensions (×5 identical)

| Component | Dimension | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Back Piece | 28.5" × 4" | Full internal width |
| Left Leg | 4" × 16.25" | With ¾" × ¾" notch on inside front corner |
| Right Leg | 4" × 16.25" | With ¾" × ¾" notch on inside front corner |
| Thickness | 0.75" | Standard MDF |

## 7. Assembly Sequence

### Phase 1: Shell Construction
1. **Back + Bottom** → L-bracket, glue & brad
2. **Add Sides** → Fit between top/bottom, glue & brad
3. **Install U-Braces** → All 5, glue to back and sides (notches facing inward)
4. **Install Side Cleats** → Continuous 44" cleats pass through leg notches
5. **Install Top/Bottom Cleats** → 20.5" cleats between legs
6. **Add Top** → Close box, glue & brad

### Phase 2: Baffle
7. **Laminate Baffle** → Two 0.75" layers, clamp 24hrs
8. **Cut Driver Holes** → 16.875" × 2
9. **Drill Bolt Holes** → 16 holes (8 per driver)
10. **Install Baffle** → Seats against continuous perimeter cleats

### Phase 3: Finishing
11. **Terminal Plate** → Back panel, seal
12. **Seal All Seams** → Silicone caulk interior
13. **Install Drivers** → Gasket tape, bolt down
