## Cutting Sequence

### Step 1: Sheet 1 - Main Box Panels
1. Crosscut sheet at 48" to separate back panel area
2. Rip remaining 48" section into: 15.25" + 15.25" + 17.5" strips
3. Crosscut side strips to 24" length
4. Save 17.5" strip for bracing

### Step 2: Sheet 2 - Baffle, Top, Bottom
1. Rip sheet into: 22.5" + 15.25" + 10.25" strips
2. Crosscut 22.5" strip at 46.5" twice for baffles
3. Crosscut 15.25" strip at 46.5" twice for top/bottom
4. Save 10.25" strip for bracing

### Step 3: Bracing from Remnants
1. Rip 3" strip for cross brace
2. Rip 2" strips for stiffeners and corner braces
3. Rip 0.75" strips for cleats
4. Crosscut all to length

### Step 4: Baffle Assembly
1. Glue and clamp baffle layers together
2. Let cure 24 hours minimum
3. Cut driver holes with hole saw or router

---

## Cutting Tips

1. **Use a track saw or table saw** with fine-tooth blade (80T) for clean cuts
2. **Support sheet fully** when cutting - plywood will sag and bind
3. **Cut baffle pieces slightly oversized** (+1/8") then trim to exact dimension after laminating
4. **Label all pieces** as you cut them
5. **Check squareness** of each piece before proceeding
6. **Sand edges** to remove splinters before assembly
