## Budget Flags

1. **Seating ($250/seat):** Tight for solid-frame couches needed for shaker mounting. May need $500-1,000 more.
2. **Dirac ($947):** Consider testing Audyssey first. If adequate, save $500-947.
3. **Projector ($5,999):** Market will shift by 2027. Budget the amount, buy last.
4. **HDMI ($80):** May be insufficient for fiber HDMI over 15+ ft for 4K/120Hz. Budget $120-150.
5. **Spare ICEpower module ($373):** Insurance against discontinuation. Consider buying or not based on risk tolerance.
