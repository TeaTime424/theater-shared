## Context

These are the four hand tools / fasteners needed to mount ICEpower 1200AS2 modules to aluminum
heatsinks or enclosure panels using M3 machine screws. M3 x 0.5mm is the standard thread pitch
for M3; the tap drill is 2.5mm and clearance is 3.2mm (≈ 1/8").

## Notes

- **Combo shortcut:** The KAKUPPA set (third row) includes both the M3 x 0.5 tap *and* 2.5mm
  drill bits together, which eliminates one separate order.
- **Drill bit note:** 3.2mm is the correct M3 clearance diameter per ISO 273 (medium fit). The
  1/8" (3.175mm) size is close enough and often sold in larger packs at lower cost — either works.
- **Screw quantity:** 100-pack is the typical minimum order for M3 SHCS on Amazon. For 8 modules
  with 4 mounting holes each, you need only ~32 screws; the remainder are useful spares.
- **Thread depth in aluminum:** 2× diameter (6mm) is minimum engagement; 8mm screws give adequate
  engagement in most 10–12mm aluminum plate.
- **Cobalt (M35) bits recommended** for aluminum heatsinks to maintain a sharp edge through
  repeated use; standard HSS works but dulls faster.
