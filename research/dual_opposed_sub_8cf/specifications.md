## Bracing System (Enhanced for Plywood)

### Cross-Braces (Interlocking)
- Quantity: 4 (2 per level)
- Dimensions: 25" × 4" × 0.75"
- Notch: 4" wide × 2" deep at center
- Position: 9.5" from top/bottom panels
- Forms + shape when interlocked

### Rib Braces
- Quantity: 16 (4 per side panel)
- Dimensions: 1.5" × 24.5" × 0.75"
- Position: 6.25" and 18.75" from panel edges

### Corner Braces (Plywood-specific)
- Quantity: 4 (one per corner)
- Dimensions: 3" × 3" L-shape, 24.5" tall
- Purpose: Reinforces weaker plywood edge joints
