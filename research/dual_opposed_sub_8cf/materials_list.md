## Tools Required

### Cutting
- Circular saw with fine-tooth blade (80+ teeth)
- Jigsaw OR router with circle jig
- Drill with bits (9/32" for bolt holes, pilot bits)

### Assembly
- Clamps (bar clamps, F-clamps)
- Square (framing square or speed square)
- Tape measure
- Pencil
- Level

### Sealing
- Paint brush or foam roller (for sealer)
- Caulk gun

### Driver Installation
- Socket wrench or nut driver (for 1/4-20 bolts)
- Torque wrench (optional, for consistent bolt tension)
