Window Brace Construction

Cut 2 Identical Braces

Window Brace — Dimensions & Cutting

Cutting the window:
1. Mark cut lines at 8" from each end
2. Drill a ½" starter hole inside the waste area
3. Insert jigsaw blade and cut along marked lines
4. Sand edges smooth

Why Window Braces? — Motor Clearance

Brace Installation Positions

Installation:
1. Mark brace positions: 9.5" from top and bottom panel inner faces
2. Apply glue to brace ends (all four contact points)
3. Slide brace into position, window centered
4. Check level
5. Brad nail through side panels into brace ends (2-3 brads per end)
