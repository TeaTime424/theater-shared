MDF Sheet Cutting Layout

2× 4' × 8' × ¾" MDF Sheets Required

Sheet 1 — Top, Bottom, and Sides

Sheet 1 yields: Top panel (with cutout), Bottom panel (with cutout), Side 1, and most of Side 2. Cut driver holes before assembly using router with circle jig.

Sheet 2 — Remaining Sides and Braces

Sheet 2 yields: Side 3, Side 4, and both window braces. Window cutouts (12" wide) in braces can be made with jigsaw after drilling starter hole.
