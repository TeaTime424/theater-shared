Corner Leg Construction

4× Hardwood Blocks — 3.5" × 3.5" × 4"

Leg Block — Dimensions

Bottom View — Leg Positions

Attachment Method — Screws from Inside

Installation sequence:
1. Position leg at corner, ½" inset from cabinet edges
2. Drill pilot holes from INSIDE cabinet (prevents splitting)
3. Drive 2× screws through bottom panel into leg
4. Drive 2× screws through side panel into leg
5. Repeat for all 4 legs
6. Attach rubber pads to leg bottoms (adhesive-backed)

Recommended: Option D (4×4 post) is ideal—actual dimensions are 3.5" × 3.5", so you just cut four 4" lengths from an 18" piece. Non-pressure-treated cedar or fir is preferred for indoor use. Sand and finish to match cabinet.
